Our Story

ightning Law was born in Seattle in 2017 right here, on this conference room table, when one lawyer decided to save trees by using scanned exhibits instead of paper ones. That lawyer, and others present, were surprised to find that the deposition went much faster than normal. This is a picture of that deposition, showing an uncluttered nearly paper free conference room table during a “paperless deposition.”

Suddenly,traveling to depositions became a lot “lighter,” armed with thumb drives instead of bankers’ boxes.

But the system was still far from perfect, not only for lawyers but also for court reporters. Lawyers wanted to spend more time with their families and less time on the road traveling to obtain testimony. Court Reporters wanted to be paid not only for their appearances but for their transcripts. Everyone seemed to be worried about a pending court reporter shortage. Lightning law was created to deliver remote, travel free testimony in a platform that ensures prompt payment for transcripts to Court Reporter. We deliver free rough transcripts to free up Court Reporters to make more appearances and to give lawyers access to testimony as quickly as possible.
